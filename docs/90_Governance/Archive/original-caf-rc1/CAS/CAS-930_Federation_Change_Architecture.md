# CAS-930 – Federation Change Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for change management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to architectural, operational, security and governance changes affecting federation participants.

## Change Principles

- Every change SHALL have an identified owner.
- Changes SHALL be assessed before implementation.
- Changes SHALL be approved through documented governance.
- Changes SHALL be traceable throughout their lifecycle.

## Change Lifecycle

Proposed → Assessed → Approved → Implemented → Validated → Closed → Archived.

## Governance

Impact assessments, approvals, implementation records and validation results SHALL be documented and retained.

## Risk and Rollback

Changes SHALL include risk assessment and, where appropriate, rollback or recovery procedures.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed change lifecycle management, approvals, traceability and validation.
