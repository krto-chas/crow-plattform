# CAS-910 – Federation Monitoring Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative monitoring architecture for federated environments governed by CAF.

## Scope

Applies to health, availability, performance, security and governance monitoring across federation participants.

## Monitoring Principles

- Monitoring SHALL be continuous for defined critical services.
- Monitoring SHALL support proactive detection.
- Monitoring SHALL be risk-based.
- Monitoring results SHALL be traceable and auditable.

## Monitoring Domains

Monitoring SHALL cover service health, trust relationships, identities, authorization, infrastructure, security events and policy compliance.

## Alert Management

Alert thresholds SHALL be documented. Alerts SHALL be classified, prioritized and routed to responsible parties.

## Metrics

Critical metrics SHOULD include availability, latency, error rates, trust status, authentication success, authorization failures and audit pipeline health.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate documented monitoring coverage, alert handling, metric governance and evidence of operational review.
