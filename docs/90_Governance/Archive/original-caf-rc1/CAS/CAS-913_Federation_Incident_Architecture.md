# CAS-913 – Federation Incident Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for incident management within CAF federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to operational, cybersecurity, privacy and trust-related incidents affecting federation participants.

## Incident Principles

- Every incident SHALL have an owner.
- Incident handling SHALL be documented.
- Evidence SHALL be preserved.
- Lessons learned SHALL feed continual improvement.

## Incident Lifecycle

Detected → Classified → Contained → Investigated → Resolved → Reviewed → Closed.

## Roles and Responsibilities

Federation participants SHALL define responsibilities for reporting, coordination, communication and recovery.

## Evidence and Reporting

Incident records SHALL be retained according to governance requirements and linked to affected trust relationships where applicable.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate documented incident governance, lifecycle management, evidence handling and post-incident review.
