# CAS-906 – Federation Identity Architecture

## Purpose

Defines normative architectural principles for identity within federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to human users, services, workloads, devices and AI agents participating in a federation.

## Identity Principles

- Every identity SHALL be unique.
- Every identity SHALL have an owner.
- Every identity SHALL have a lifecycle.
- Identities SHALL be auditable.

## Identity Lifecycle

Requested → Issued → Active → Suspended → Revoked → Archived.

## Identity Types

Human, Service, Device, Workload, AI Agent, Organization.

## Requirements

Identity issuance, modification and revocation SHALL follow documented governance.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate traceable identity management and lifecycle controls.
