# CAS-937 – Federation Performance Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for performance management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to applications, platforms, infrastructure, networks and services requiring measurable performance objectives.

## Performance Principles

- Performance objectives SHALL align with business requirements.
- Performance SHALL be measurable and continuously monitored.
- Performance bottlenecks SHALL be identified and addressed.
- Optimization activities SHALL be documented and repeatable.

## Performance Lifecycle

Defined → Measured → Analyzed → Optimized → Validated → Reviewed.

## Governance

Performance baselines, thresholds, ownership and review cycles SHALL be governed through documented federation processes.

## Monitoring and Optimization

Implementations SHOULD include latency, throughput, resource utilization, scalability analysis and trend reporting.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed performance management, monitoring, optimization and continual improvement.
