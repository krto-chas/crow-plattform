# CAS-939 – Federation Reliability Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for reliability management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to services, applications, infrastructure, integrations and operational processes that require reliable operation.

## Reliability Principles

- Reliability SHALL support business and mission objectives.
- Failure modes SHALL be identified and assessed.
- Reliability metrics SHALL be monitored.
- Continuous improvement SHALL be based on operational evidence.

## Reliability Lifecycle

Planned → Designed → Validated → Operated → Measured → Improved → Retired.

## Governance

Reliability objectives, ownership, review intervals and improvement actions SHALL follow documented federation governance.

## Engineering Practices

Implementations SHOULD include fault tolerance, redundancy, error budgeting, resilience testing and post-incident reviews.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed reliability management, measurable objectives, monitoring and continual improvement.
