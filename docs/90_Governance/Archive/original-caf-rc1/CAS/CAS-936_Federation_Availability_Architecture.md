# CAS-936 – Federation Availability Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for ensuring availability across CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to services, platforms, infrastructure and supporting capabilities requiring defined availability objectives.

## Availability Principles

- Availability SHALL align with business and mission requirements.
- Single points of failure SHOULD be minimized.
- Availability SHALL be continuously monitored.
- Planned and unplanned outages SHALL be documented and reviewed.

## Availability Lifecycle

Planned → Implemented → Monitored → Measured → Improved → Retired.

## Governance

Availability objectives, ownership, maintenance windows and review cycles SHALL be governed through documented federation processes.

## Resilience and Measurement

Implementations SHOULD include redundancy, failover, health monitoring, recovery testing and availability reporting.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed availability management, measurable objectives, monitoring and continual improvement.
