# CAS-931 – Federation Dependency Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for dependency management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to business, application, infrastructure, service and external dependencies affecting federation participants.

## Dependency Principles

- Every critical dependency SHALL be identified.
- Dependencies SHALL have an accountable owner.
- Dependency relationships SHALL be documented.
- Critical dependencies SHALL be periodically reviewed.

## Dependency Lifecycle

Identified → Assessed → Approved → Managed → Reviewed → Retired → Archived.

## Governance

Dependency changes, risk assessments and ownership updates SHALL follow documented federation governance.

## Mapping and Analysis

Dependency maps SHOULD support impact analysis, resilience planning and change management.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed dependency lifecycle management, ownership, traceability and periodic review.
