# CAS-904 – Architecture Governance

## Purpose

Define the mandatory governance requirements for architecture activities, decisions and controlled artefacts within Crow Architecture Framework (CAF).

## Scope

Applies to all architecture governance processes, decision forums, roles and governed artefacts managed under CAF.

## Governance Principles

Architecture governance SHALL be accountable, transparent, traceable, risk-based and consistently applied.

## Roles and Responsibilities

Governance SHALL define accountable roles for ownership, review, approval, publication and lifecycle management of architecture artefacts.

## Decision Governance

Significant architecture decisions SHALL be documented, approved by the appropriate authority and traceable through Architecture Decision Records (ADR).

## Exception Management

Requests to deviate from applicable standards SHALL be documented, assessed, approved or rejected and periodically reviewed.

## Review Requirements

Governed artefacts SHALL undergo formal review before approval and publication. Review outcomes SHALL be retained as governance evidence.

## Conformance

A CAF-compliant governance implementation SHALL satisfy the mandatory governance, decision, review and exception management requirements defined by this standard.

## Summary

Architecture Governance establishes the mandatory control framework that ensures consistent decision-making, accountability and stewardship across CAF.
