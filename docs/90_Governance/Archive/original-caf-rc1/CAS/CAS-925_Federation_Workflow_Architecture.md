# CAS-925 – Federation Workflow Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for workflows executed within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to business, operational and governance workflows spanning one or more federation participants.

## Workflow Principles

- Workflows SHALL have an identified owner.
- Workflow states SHALL be explicitly defined.
- Workflow execution SHALL be traceable.
- Workflow changes SHALL follow federation governance.

## Workflow Lifecycle

Defined → Approved → Implemented → Executed → Monitored → Improved → Retired.

## Governance

Workflow definitions, responsibilities, decision points and exception handling SHALL be documented and version controlled.

## Execution and Monitoring

Workflow execution SHALL support auditability, operational monitoring and documented recovery from failures.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed workflow lifecycle management, ownership, traceability and monitoring.
