# CAS-915 – Federation Continuity Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for business and operational continuity within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to continuity planning, service restoration, dependency management and governance across federation participants.

## Continuity Principles

- Critical services SHALL have documented continuity plans.
- Dependencies SHALL be identified.
- Recovery priorities SHALL be risk-based.
- Continuity capabilities SHALL be exercised periodically.

## Continuity Planning

Continuity plans SHALL define responsibilities, communication paths, recovery objectives and minimum operational capability.

## Dependency Management

Internal and external dependencies SHALL be documented, reviewed and considered in continuity planning.

## Review and Testing

Continuity plans SHALL be reviewed after significant changes and validated through exercises at defined intervals.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate approved continuity plans, governance, testing records and continual improvement.
