# CAS-911 – Federation Policy Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for policy management in CAF federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to security, operational, governance and compliance policies affecting federation participants.

## Policy Principles

- Policies SHALL have a defined owner.
- Policies SHALL be version controlled.
- Policies SHALL be reviewable.
- Policies SHALL be traceable to business or regulatory requirements.

## Policy Lifecycle

Draft → Review → Approved → Published → Active → Deprecated → Retired.

## Policy Governance

Policy changes SHALL be approved through documented governance and recorded for audit.

## Policy Distribution

Participants SHALL have access to the current approved policy set. Superseded policies SHALL be retained according to retention requirements.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate policy ownership, lifecycle management, traceability and governance.
