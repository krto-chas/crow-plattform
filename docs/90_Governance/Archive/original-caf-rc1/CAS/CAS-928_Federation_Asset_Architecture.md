# CAS-928 – Federation Asset Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for managing assets within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to business, information, software, infrastructure and service assets managed by federation participants.

## Asset Principles

- Every asset SHALL have an identified owner.
- Assets SHALL be uniquely identifiable.
- Asset lifecycle SHALL be governed.
- Asset relationships SHALL be documented and traceable.

## Asset Lifecycle

Registered → Classified → Active → Modified → Reviewed → Retired → Archived.

## Governance

Asset ownership, classification, dependencies and changes SHALL follow documented federation governance.

## Inventory and Relationships

Asset inventories SHALL be maintained and relationships to services, capabilities and processes SHOULD be documented.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed asset lifecycle management, ownership, inventory management and traceability.
