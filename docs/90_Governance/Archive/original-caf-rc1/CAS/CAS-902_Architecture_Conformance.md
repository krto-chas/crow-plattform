# CAS-902 – Architecture Conformance

## Purpose

Define the normative requirements for assessing conformance with the Crow Architecture Framework (CAF) and Crow Architecture Standards (CAS).

## Scope

Applies to all governed architecture artefacts, repositories, methods and implementations claiming CAF conformance.

## Conformance Principles

Conformance SHALL be objective, repeatable, evidence-based and traceable.

## Assessment Criteria

Assessments SHALL verify compliance with applicable architecture principles, standards, governance requirements, lifecycle requirements and traceability obligations.

## Evidence

Conformance claims SHALL be supported by documented evidence including approved artefacts, review records, Architecture Decision Records (ADR) and compliance reports.

## Non-conformities

Non-conformities SHALL be documented, classified by impact, assigned an owner and tracked until resolved or formally accepted.

## Reporting

Assessment results SHALL identify the assessed scope, findings, evidence, deviations and overall conformance status.

## Summary

Architecture Conformance provides a consistent mechanism for demonstrating and verifying adherence to CAF.
