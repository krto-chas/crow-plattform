# CAS-934 – Federation Service Level Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for service level management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to service level objectives (SLOs), agreements (SLAs), operational level agreements (OLAs), and performance commitments across federation participants.

## Service Level Principles

- Service levels SHALL align with business objectives.
- SLOs and SLAs SHALL be measurable.
- Performance SHALL be continuously monitored.
- Deviations SHALL trigger documented review and corrective action.

## Service Level Lifecycle

Defined → Approved → Monitored → Reported → Reviewed → Updated → Retired.

## Governance

Service level definitions, ownership, thresholds and review intervals SHALL be governed through documented federation processes.

## Performance and Reporting

Performance reports SHOULD include availability, reliability, response times, capacity trends and improvement actions.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed service level management, measurable objectives, monitoring and periodic review.
