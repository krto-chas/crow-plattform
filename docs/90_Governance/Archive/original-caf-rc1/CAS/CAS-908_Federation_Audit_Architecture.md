# CAS-908 – Federation Audit Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative audit architecture for federated environments governed by CAF.

## Scope

Applies to all audit events, logs, evidence and compliance activities within a federation.

## Audit Principles

- Audit records SHALL be complete.
- Audit records SHALL be tamper-evident.
- Audit records SHALL be traceable.
- Audit processes SHALL support independent review.

## Audit Sources

Identity, authorization, trust lifecycle, policy changes, administrative actions and security events SHALL generate auditable records where applicable.

## Evidence Management

Evidence SHALL be retained according to governance requirements and protected against unauthorized modification or deletion.

## Audit Lifecycle

Generated → Collected → Protected → Reviewed → Archived → Disposed.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate complete audit coverage, evidence integrity and traceable governance.
