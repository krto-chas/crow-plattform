# CAS-927 – Federation Capability Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for capability management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to business, operational, security and technology capabilities owned or shared by federation participants.

## Capability Principles

- Every capability SHALL have an identified owner.
- Capabilities SHALL support defined business objectives.
- Capability dependencies SHALL be documented.
- Capability maturity SHOULD be periodically assessed.

## Capability Lifecycle

Identified → Assessed → Planned → Implemented → Operational → Improved → Retired.

## Governance

Capability ownership, funding, dependencies and changes SHALL follow documented federation governance.

## Assessment and Improvement

Capabilities SHALL define measurable objectives and support continual improvement through regular reviews.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed capability lifecycle management, ownership, dependency management and periodic assessment.
