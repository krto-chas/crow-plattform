# CAS-912 – Federation Risk Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for identifying, assessing, treating and monitoring risks within CAF federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to strategic, operational, security, privacy and compliance risks affecting federation participants.

## Risk Principles

- Risks SHALL have an assigned owner.
- Risk decisions SHALL be documented.
- Risk treatment SHALL be traceable.
- Risk acceptance SHALL follow documented governance.

## Risk Lifecycle

Identified → Assessed → Prioritized → Treated → Monitored → Closed.

## Risk Treatment

Treatment options MAY include mitigation, transfer, avoidance or acceptance. Selected treatments SHALL be documented and reviewed.

## Risk Reporting

Material risks SHALL be reported through federation governance. Risk registers SHALL be maintained and periodically reviewed.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate documented risk governance, lifecycle management, ownership and review.
