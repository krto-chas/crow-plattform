# CAS-905 – Federation Trust Architecture

## Purpose

Define the normative architecture for establishing, maintaining and revoking trust relationships within federated environments governed by CAF.

## Scope

Applies to organizations, systems, services and AI participants operating within a CAF-governed federation.

## Federation Roles

Federation Authority, Trust Authority, Federation Participant, Service Provider and Consumer SHALL have defined responsibilities.

## Trust Domains

Trust Domains SHALL isolate trust boundaries. Cross-domain trust SHALL be explicitly established and documented.

## Trust Lifecycle

Requested → Verified → Approved → Active → Suspended → Revoked → Archived.

## Security Requirements

Participants SHALL have verifiable identities. Authentication, authorization, auditability, integrity and incident handling SHALL be governed by documented controls.

## Reference Architecture

The logical architecture consists of governance authorities, trust domains, federation participants and services connected through governed trust relationships.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL document trust relationships, governance, lifecycle state and security controls to claim conformance.

## Summary

CAS-905 establishes the normative trust architecture supporting secure, governed and traceable federation.
