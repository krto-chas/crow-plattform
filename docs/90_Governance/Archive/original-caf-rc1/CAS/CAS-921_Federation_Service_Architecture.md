# CAS-921 – Federation Service Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for services operating within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to business services, platform services, infrastructure services and shared federation services.

## Service Principles

- Every service SHALL have an identified owner.
- Every service SHALL publish its interface and dependencies.
- Services SHALL define service objectives and operational responsibilities.
- Services SHALL support traceability throughout their lifecycle.

## Service Lifecycle

Proposed → Designed → Approved → Implemented → Operational → Deprecated → Retired.

## Service Governance

Service ownership, changes, dependencies and operational status SHALL be governed through documented processes.

## Dependencies

Critical dependencies SHALL be identified, documented and periodically reviewed to reduce operational risk.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed service lifecycle management, ownership, dependency management and operational traceability.
