# CAS-916 – Federation Compliance Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for compliance management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to regulatory, contractual, internal and framework compliance obligations across federation participants.

## Compliance Principles

- Compliance obligations SHALL be identified.
- Compliance SHALL have assigned ownership.
- Evidence SHALL be maintained.
- Compliance status SHALL be periodically reviewed.

## Compliance Lifecycle

Identified → Assessed → Implemented → Verified → Reported → Improved.

## Evidence Management

Evidence SHALL be complete, traceable, protected against unauthorized modification and retained according to governance requirements.

## Reporting and Governance

Compliance reports SHALL be reviewed by the appropriate governance authority. Non-conformities SHALL be documented, prioritized and tracked until resolved.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate documented compliance obligations, evidence management, governance oversight and continual improvement.
