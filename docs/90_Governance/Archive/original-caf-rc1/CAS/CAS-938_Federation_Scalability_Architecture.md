# CAS-938 – Federation Scalability Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for scalability management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to services, applications, infrastructure, platforms and integrations that must scale to meet changing demand.

## Scalability Principles

- Scalability SHALL support business and mission objectives.
- Horizontal and vertical scaling options SHOULD be evaluated.
- Capacity growth SHALL be predictable and measurable.
- Scaling events SHALL be monitored and documented.

## Scalability Lifecycle

Planned → Designed → Implemented → Monitored → Optimized → Reviewed.

## Governance

Scalability objectives, ownership, design decisions and review cycles SHALL follow documented federation governance.

## Elasticity and Growth

Implementations SHOULD support automated scaling, workload balancing, demand forecasting and resilience during growth.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed scalability management, measurable objectives, monitoring and continual improvement.
