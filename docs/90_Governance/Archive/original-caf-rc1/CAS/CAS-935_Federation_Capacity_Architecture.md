# CAS-935 – Federation Capacity Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for capacity management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to planning, monitoring and optimization of compute, storage, network and service capacity across federation participants.

## Capacity Principles

- Capacity SHALL support agreed service levels.
- Capacity planning SHALL be risk-informed.
- Utilization SHALL be continuously monitored.
- Forecasts SHALL be periodically reviewed and updated.

## Capacity Lifecycle

Forecasted → Planned → Provisioned → Monitored → Optimized → Retired.

## Governance

Capacity plans, thresholds, ownership and review cycles SHALL be governed through documented federation processes.

## Monitoring and Forecasting

Monitoring SHOULD include utilization trends, saturation indicators, demand forecasts and scalability assessments.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed capacity planning, monitoring, forecasting and continual improvement.
