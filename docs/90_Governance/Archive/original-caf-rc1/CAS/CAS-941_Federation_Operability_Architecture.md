# CAS-941 – Federation Operability Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for operability within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to the operational management of services, platforms, infrastructure, applications and supporting capabilities.

## Operability Principles

- Operational procedures SHALL be documented.
- Roles and responsibilities SHALL be clearly assigned.
- Operational health SHALL be continuously monitored.
- Operational improvements SHALL be based on measurable outcomes.

## Operability Lifecycle

Designed → Deployed → Operated → Monitored → Improved → Retired.

## Governance

Operational policies, procedures, ownership and review cycles SHALL be governed through documented federation processes.

## Operational Readiness

Implementations SHOULD include runbooks, standard operating procedures, health checks, automation, training and operational reviews.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed operability management, documented operational practices and continual improvement.
