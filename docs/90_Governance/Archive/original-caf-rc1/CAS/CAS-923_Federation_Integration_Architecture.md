# CAS-923 – Federation Integration Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for integrations within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to synchronous, asynchronous and hybrid integrations between federation participants.

## Integration Principles

- Integrations SHALL have an identified owner.
- Integration contracts SHALL be documented.
- Integrations SHALL preserve traceability across trust domains.
- Changes SHALL follow federation governance.

## Integration Lifecycle

Proposed → Designed → Approved → Implemented → Operational → Deprecated → Retired.

## Integration Governance

Interfaces, dependencies, transformations and compatibility requirements SHALL be version-controlled and reviewed.

## Reliability and Observability

Integrations SHALL define error handling, retries, monitoring and operational responsibilities appropriate to their criticality.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed integration lifecycle management, ownership, traceability and operational monitoring.
