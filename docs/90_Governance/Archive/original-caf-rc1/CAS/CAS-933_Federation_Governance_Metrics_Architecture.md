# CAS-933 – Federation Governance Metrics Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for governance metrics within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to metrics, KPIs and governance indicators used to evaluate federation effectiveness and compliance.

## Principles

- Every metric SHALL have a defined owner.
- Metrics SHALL have documented calculation methods.
- Metrics SHALL be periodically reviewed.
- Governance decisions SHOULD be supported by objective measurements.

## Metric Lifecycle

Defined → Approved → Collected → Reported → Reviewed → Retired.

## Governance

Metric definitions, thresholds and reporting responsibilities SHALL be version controlled and approved.

## Reporting

Reports SHALL identify trends, deviations and recommended actions appropriate to the governance process.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed metric definitions, reporting, traceability and periodic review.
