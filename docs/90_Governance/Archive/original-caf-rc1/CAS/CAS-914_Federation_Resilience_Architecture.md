# CAS-914 – Federation Resilience Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for resilience across CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to resilience planning, continuity, recovery and adaptive operation for federation participants.

## Resilience Principles

- Critical capabilities SHALL be identified.
- Single points of failure SHOULD be minimized.
- Recovery objectives SHALL be documented.
- Resilience SHALL be periodically validated.

## Capability Planning

Critical services SHALL have documented dependencies, recovery priorities and continuity requirements.

## Recovery Lifecycle

Prepared → Disrupted → Responding → Recovering → Restored → Improved.

## Validation

Recovery procedures SHALL be exercised at defined intervals and results SHALL be reviewed through governance.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate resilience planning, recovery governance, testing and continual improvement.
