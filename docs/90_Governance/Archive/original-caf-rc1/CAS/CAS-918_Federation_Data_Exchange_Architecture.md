# CAS-918 – Federation Data Exchange Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for secure and governed data exchange within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to structured and unstructured data exchanged between federation participants.

## Data Exchange Principles

- Data exchanges SHALL be explicitly authorized.
- Data provenance SHALL be maintained.
- Data integrity SHALL be protected.
- Exchanges SHALL be auditable.

## Exchange Model

Each exchange SHALL define the sender, receiver, purpose, information classification, applicable policies and accountability.

## Trust Boundaries

Cross-domain exchanges SHALL comply with federation trust requirements and approved governance processes.

## Lifecycle

Requested → Approved → Established → Active → Modified → Terminated → Archived.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed data exchange, traceability, integrity protection and lifecycle management.
