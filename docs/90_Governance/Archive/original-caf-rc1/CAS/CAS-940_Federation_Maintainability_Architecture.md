# CAS-940 – Federation Maintainability Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for maintainability within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to services, applications, infrastructure, integrations and operational components requiring sustainable maintenance.

## Maintainability Principles

- Maintainability SHALL be considered throughout the lifecycle.
- Components SHALL be modular where practical.
- Maintenance activities SHALL be documented and traceable.
- Technical debt SHOULD be monitored and managed.

## Maintainability Lifecycle

Designed → Implemented → Maintained → Improved → Modernized → Retired.

## Governance

Maintenance plans, ownership, review intervals and technical debt management SHALL follow documented federation governance.

## Operational Practices

Implementations SHOULD include preventive maintenance, patch management, documentation updates, dependency reviews and automation where appropriate.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed maintainability management, documented maintenance processes and continual improvement.
