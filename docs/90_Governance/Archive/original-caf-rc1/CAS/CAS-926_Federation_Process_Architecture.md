# CAS-926 – Federation Process Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for processes operating within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to business, operational, security and governance processes executed across federation participants.

## Process Principles

- Every process SHALL have an identified owner.
- Process objectives SHALL be documented.
- Inputs, outputs and controls SHALL be defined.
- Process performance SHALL be measurable and reviewable.

## Process Lifecycle

Defined → Approved → Implemented → Operational → Measured → Improved → Retired.

## Governance

Process changes, responsibilities and performance reviews SHALL follow documented federation governance.

## Performance and Improvement

Processes SHALL define performance indicators and support continual improvement through periodic review.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed process lifecycle management, ownership, measurement and continual improvement.
