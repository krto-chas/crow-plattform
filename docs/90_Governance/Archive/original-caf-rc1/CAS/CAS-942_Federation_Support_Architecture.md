# CAS-942 – Federation Support Architecture

## Purpose

Defines the normative architecture for support management within CAF-governed federated environments.

## Scope

Applies to service desk functions, operational support, technical support, escalation paths and user assistance.

## Support Principles

- Support services SHALL have defined ownership.
- Incidents and requests SHALL be tracked.
- Escalation paths SHALL be documented.
- Knowledge SHALL be captured and reused.

## Support Lifecycle

Requested → Logged → Triaged → Resolved → Verified → Closed → Reviewed.

## Governance

Support policies, SLAs, ownership and review processes SHALL be governed through documented federation procedures.

## Knowledge and Improvement

Implementations SHOULD maintain a knowledge base, analyze recurring issues, measure support performance and drive continual improvement.

## Conformance

Implementations SHALL demonstrate governed support processes, traceability, measurable performance and continual improvement.
