# CAF-007 – Capability Framework

## Purpose

Define how business capabilities are identified, described, governed and related to architecture within CAF.

## Scope

The capability framework applies to business, information, application, technology and supporting capabilities used in governed architectures.

## Capability Definition

A capability represents an organization's ability to achieve a defined outcome independent of organizational structure or technical implementation.

## Capability Attributes

- Identifier
- Name
- Description
- Owner
- Status
- Version
- Business Outcome
- Related Processes
- Supporting Information
- Supporting Components

## Capability Relationships

Capabilities may realize business objectives, depend on other capabilities and be realized by processes, information, applications and technology.

## Governance

Capabilities shall have an assigned owner, documented lifecycle, traceable relationships and periodic review.

## Conformance

A CAF-compliant capability model shall use unique identifiers, documented ownership, lifecycle status and explicit relationships.

## Summary

The Capability Framework connects business intent with architectural implementation and provides a stable planning model independent of organizational changes.
