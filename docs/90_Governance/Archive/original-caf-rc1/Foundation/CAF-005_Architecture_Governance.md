# CAF-005 – Architecture Governance

## Purpose

Define the governance model for Crow Architecture Framework (CAF), including roles, responsibilities, decision making, reviews and compliance.

## Governance Principles

- Governance shall support architecture, not hinder it.
- Significant decisions shall be documented.
- Accountability shall be explicit.
- Governance shall be risk-based and proportionate.

## Roles

Architecture Board
Chief Architect
Domain Architect
Solution Architect
Artefact Owner
Project Sponsor

## Decision Process

Proposal → Review → Architecture Board Decision → Publication → Implementation → Verification

## Reviews

Architecture reviews shall verify conformance with CAF principles, CAS standards, traceability and documented decisions.

## Compliance

Non-conformities shall be documented, assessed, approved or remediated through the governance process.

## Summary

Architecture Governance provides the decision structure that ensures consistency, accountability and controlled evolution of the architecture.
