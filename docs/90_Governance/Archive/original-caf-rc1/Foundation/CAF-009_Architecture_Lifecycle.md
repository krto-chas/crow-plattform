# CAF-009 – Architecture Lifecycle

## Purpose

Define the lifecycle model governing architecture artefacts throughout their existence within CAF.

## Lifecycle Objectives

- Consistent lifecycle management
- Controlled evolution
- Traceability
- Governance

## Lifecycle States

Draft
Review
Approved
Released
Deprecated
Archived

## State Transitions

Transitions between lifecycle states shall be governed by approved review and decision processes. Each transition shall be recorded.

## Lifecycle Activities

Creation, review, approval, publication, maintenance, revision, deprecation and archival shall be defined for every governed artefact.

## Version Management

Every lifecycle transition resulting in content changes shall update the artefact version in accordance with the versioning policy.

## Retention and Archival

Archived artefacts shall remain available for audit and historical traceability but shall no longer be modified.

## Conformance

A CAF-compliant implementation shall manage artefacts using the defined lifecycle states and preserve complete lifecycle history.

## Summary

The Architecture Lifecycle provides a controlled and traceable process for managing architecture artefacts from creation through archival.
